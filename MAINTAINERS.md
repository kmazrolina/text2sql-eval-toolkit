## Becoming a Maintainer

Maintainers are active contributors who have demonstrated:
- Consistent contributions to the project
- Deep understanding of the codebase
- Commitment to the project's goals and community
- Ability to review and provide constructive feedback on pull requests

If you're interested in becoming a maintainer, please reach out to the current maintainers or open an issue to discuss.
